Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TeachBest ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our WhatsApp-based revision service.

1. Who we are

TeachBest is an educational service that provides AP exam revision materials via WhatsApp.

2. Information we collect

When you use our WhatsApp service, we collect:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number, used to identify your account and send you revision content
  • Messages you send to our WhatsApp number, used to process your requests
  • Credit balance and usage history associated with your phone number

We do not collect, store, or process any payment data. All payments are handled entirely by third-party payment providers. We have no access to your card details or any financial information.

3. How we use your information

We use your information solely to:

  • Deliver AP revision slide decks and materials you request
  • Manage your credit balance
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve the service

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data storage

Your phone number and usage data are stored securely on our servers. We retain this data for as long as you use the service. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. WhatsApp

Our service operates via the WhatsApp Business API. Your use of WhatsApp is also subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by stopping use of the service

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via teachbest.uk.

7. Cookies

This website does not use cookies.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us via teachbest.uk.
